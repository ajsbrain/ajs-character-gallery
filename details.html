Goku

Backstory: Goku is a Saiyan sent to Earth as a baby who grows up to become one of the strongest warriors in the universe.

Abilities: Kamehameha, Super Saiyan transformations, Ultra Instinct, flight.

Personality: Brave, kind-hearted, loves training and fighting strong opponents.
